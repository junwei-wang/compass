About Me

I am a Ph.D student at CryptoExperts under the supervision of Jean-Sébastien CORON, Sihem MESNAGER, Pascal PAILLIER, and Matthieu RIVAIN. I am also an ECRYPT-NET fellow and received funding from the European Union's Horizon 2020 research and innovation programme under the Marie Skłodowska-Curie grant agreement No. 643161.

My research interest is white-box cryptography. I co-organized the 2nd edition of the WhibOx contest, which is the CTF event of CHES 2019.
