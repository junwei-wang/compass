About Me

I am a Ph.D student at CryptoExperts under the supervision of Jean-Sébastien CORON, Sihem MESNAGER, Pascal PAILLIER, and Matthieu RIVAIN. I am also an ECRYPT-NET fellow and receive funding from the European Union’s Horizon 2020 research and innovation programme under the Marie Skłodowska-Curie grant agreement No. 643161.

My research interest is white-box cryptography.
